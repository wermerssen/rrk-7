Component 6 — Architectural Layer

The Architectural Layer represents the highest structural tier within the RRK‑7.0 framework. It integrates recursive, emergent, meta‑structural, convergent, and transformative processes into a coherent system architecture.

Structural Role

This layer defines the overarching order in which all components interact. It ensures that recursive units, meta‑structures, emergence modules, convergence operators, and transformation cores remain functionally aligned.

Functional Significance

The Architectural Layer enables the full expression of the RRK‑7.0 architecture by stabilizing the interactions of lower layers and translating them into a unified systemic logic.
