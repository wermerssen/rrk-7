Evolution of the RRK Framework

The evolution of the RRK framework describes the development of recursive meta‑structures across different versions. Each version represents a structural refinement, extension, or reorganization of the underlying theoretical architecture.

RRK Versions

  • RRK‑1.x — initial recursive structure definitions
  • RRK‑2.x — introduction of meta‑structural layers
  • RRK‑3.x — emergence mechanisms and system dynamics
  • RRK‑4.x — convergence models and stability criteria
  • RRK‑5.x — interaction and coupling structures
  • RRK‑6.x — formal preregistrations and OSF integration
  • RRK‑7.x — current version, consolidated meta‑structure
